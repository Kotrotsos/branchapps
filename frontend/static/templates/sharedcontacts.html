Shared Contacts

Easily share your Google Contacts with your colleagues right from your own google contacts interface.

  • Share your contacts with individual users or groups within your organisation
  • 2-way near real-time sync
  • Give colleagues edit or just reading rights
  • Change your shared contact information right from your google contacts
  • Have always a backup of your shared contacts online

How does it work:

  • Domain administrator add BranchApps Shared contacts to the domain
  • Domain administrator add a shared group and gives rights (read or edit) to people within the domain
  • People with Edit rights can add their contacts to the group wich will automatically be visible in de contacts of people who have read or edit rights
  • Changes to contact information can be done right from Google Contacts by the person who had edit rights and who is the owner of that specific contact person

With the shared contacts app you can easily create shared groups to share your contacts within your domain.
